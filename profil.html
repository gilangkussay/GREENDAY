Billie Joe Armstrong

Vocalist and Guitarist

Billie Joe Armstrong is the lead vocalist, primary songwriter, and guitarist of Green Day. He co-founded the band in 1986 and has been the frontman ever since.

Mike Dirnt

Bassist

Mike Dirnt, the band's bassist, has been with Green Day since its inception. He is known for his energetic stage presence and melodic bass lines.

Tré Cool

Drummer

Tré Cool, Green Day's drummer, joined the band in 1990. His fast-paced drumming and quirky personality have made him a fan favorite.
